Privacy Policy for MAVVision

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

MAVVision ("the App") is provided by devararendy as a tool for connecting MAVLink telemetry and streaming camera data. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for anyone using our service.

1. Information Collection and Use

MAVVision is designed as a "bridge" application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All data processing (MAVLink telemetry and Video Streaming) happens locally on your device or is transmitted directly to the IP addresses/ports specified by you.

2. Permissions and Data Usage

To provide its core functionality, especially when the device is attached to a Remote Controlled (RC) vehicle, the App requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used to capture video for the FPV (First Person View) stream. Video is processed in real-time and only recorded to local storage if explicitly triggered by the user.
  • Microphone (Foreground & Background): Required for capturing audio to be synchronized with the video stream and local recordings. Because the app is often used while the phone is attached to an RC vehicle, microphone access must remain active in the foreground/background to ensure uninterrupted audio capture based on user configuration.
  • Location (Foreground Service): Used solely to access system APIs for checking WiFi and Cellular signal levels. This data is displayed on the On-Screen Display (OSD) to ensure the operator is aware of signal drops while the phone is operating on the vehicle. Precise location data is NOT collected, tracked, or shared.
  • Network (Local & Internet): Used to establish TCP/UDP connections for MAVLink telemetry and video streaming.
  • USB/Serial: Used to communicate with flight controllers or telemetry radios via USB-OTG.
  • In-App Purchases (Billing): Used to process transactions for "Pro" features via the Google Play Store.
